NOTES
-----

1. All WDF elements and DSP filters are noexcept and use single-precision float
   (1.0f not 1.0). No heap allocation or exceptions.

2. Parametric families (WdfDiodeFamily, WdfNpnBjtFamily, WdfPnpBjtFamily,
   WdfJfetFamily, WdfMosfetFamily) are reusable templates that accept
   device parameters at compile time. Preset constants are defined in
   constexpr namespace blocks.

3. Op-amp models (LM741, LM308, JRC4558) are NOT WDF elements — they are
   signal processing blocks that model slew rate, rail clamping, and
   frequency response without wave-based scattering.

4. Two known test failures identified in baseline (2026-03-25):
   - pnp_bjt_test.cpp: suspected Jacobian cross-partial error
   - wah_test.cpp: suspected pot update not propagating
   Both test files now compile successfully and are marked for audit/repair.

5. New DSP primitives added since 2026-03-28:
   - BiquadFilter (8 types: LP, HP, BP, BS, Peaking, LS, HS, APF)
   - StateVariableFilter (VA-modeled SVF with soft saturation)
